“Beauty Behind the Madness, the second studio album by the Weeknd, emerged as a significant milestone in his career. Released on August 28, 2015, through XO and Republic Records, the album showcased a fusion of talent, featuring guest appearances from artists like Labrinth, Ed Sheeran, and Lana Del Rey. Executive produced by the Weeknd, DaHeala, and Illangelo, the album boasted an impressive lineup of producers, including Kanye West and Max Martin. Five singles, including “The Hills” and “Can’t Feel My Face,” reached number-one on the US Billboard Hot 100, while “Earned It” earned an Academy Award nomination.
