“Dawn FM, the fifth studio album by the Weeknd, emerged on January 7, 2022, captivating listeners with its enthralling concept of traversing a state of purgatory towards the ‘light at the end of a tunnel.’ Featuring narration by Jim Carrey, guest vocals from Tyler, the Creator and Lil Wayne, and spoken word appearances from Quincy Jones and Josh Safdie, the album showcased the Weeknd’s diverse collaborations and artistry. Musically, the record delved into upbeat dance-pop and synth-pop, drawing inspiration from 1980s new wave, funk, and electronic dance music. Earning critical acclaim, Dawn FM reached number one in over 10 countries and secured a number two spot on the US Billboard 200 chart, further solidifying the Weeknd’s musical legacy.”
