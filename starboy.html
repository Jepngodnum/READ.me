“Starboy, the third studio album by Canadian singer the Weeknd, was released on November 25, 2016, under the XO and Republic Records labels. The album boasts an impressive lineup of guest appearances, including Daft Punk, Lana Del Rey, Kendrick Lamar, and Future. As executive producers, the Weeknd and Doc McKinney collaborated with renowned producers like Diplo, Cashmere Cat, Metro Boomin, Frank Dukes, and Labrinth. The album enjoyed great success, with seven hit singles, including the chart-topping “Starboy” and “Die for You”. Certified four-times platinum by the RIAA, “Starboy” remains a standout milestone in the Weeknd’s career.”
