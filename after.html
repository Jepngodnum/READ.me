“After Hours, the fourth studio album by Canadian singer the Weeknd, took the music world by storm when it was released on March 20, 2020, under XO and Republic Records. Showcasing the Weeknd’s exceptional artistry, the album delves into themes of loneliness, self-loathing, and escapism. Departing from his previous work, After Hours introduced new wave and dream pop influences, earning critical acclaim as an artistic reinvention. The album’s singles, including “Blinding Lights,” “Heartless,” and “Save Your Tears,” topped the Billboard Hot 100, further solidifying the Weeknd’s prominence. With numerous accolades and chart-topping success, After Hours continues to leave an indelible mark on the music industry.”
